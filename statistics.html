What is Health Equity and Inequities?

Health Equity is where everyone in a community has the proper accesss to health care thats not being disadvantaed from social position, background, and circumstances. In our current society, health care is expensive and there is a rise of health inequities. For this to change, social policies need to be address and there needs to be a movement to make health care more accessible. To read more visit de Beaumont.
