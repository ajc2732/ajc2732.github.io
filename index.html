My Mission

My name is Alice Cauchi, and I created health empowerment to allow people to easily access healthcare resources. Insurance isn't cheap, and in America, the government healthcare system is not accessible to everybody. Health Empowerment allows for people of all backgrounds and financial situations to find the resources and information they need about healthcare options in the Rochester area.
